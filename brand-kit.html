04 — Voice

How We Talk

  • Confident, never corny. Premium without being stuffy.
  • Short, punchy lines. Say it like you'd say it to a friend who has taste.
  • Lean on the hooks: hand-picked heat, curated, plug pricing, stack the cart, save more.
  • Ships nationwide — fast and tracked.
  • Low pressure: "send your order, pay when it's confirmed."
05 — Usage

Do & Don't

Do

  • Gold on jet black is the signature — use it everywhere.
  • Keep lots of negative space; let it feel premium.
  • Use the star mark alone as a social avatar / favicon.
  • Shoot product on clean, consistent backgrounds.

Don't

  • Don't put gold text on white — use the on-light logo instead.
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or add drop shadows to the logo.
  • Don't swap the fonts or use more than two.
  • Don't crowd the mark with other graphics.
